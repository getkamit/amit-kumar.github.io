Research

My research explores a wide array of topics in theoretical and computational fluid dynamics:

  • Shock wave propagation and mechanical effects from intense explosions
  • Molecular dynamics simulations of non-equilibrium systems
  • Continuum theories: Euler and Navier-Stokes equations
  • Non-equilibrium fluid mechanics and fluctuations
  • Active systems
  • Ring and linear polymers (charged/uncharged)
