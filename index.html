About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the International Centre for Theoretical Sciences (ICTS), Bangalore. I recently completed my PhD at The Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai. My research lies at the intersection of theoretical and computational physics, focusing on complex fluid systems and gas dynamics.

Research Interests

  • Shock propagation from intense explosions using molecular dynamics simulations
  • Theoretical fluid mechanics via Euler and Navier-Stokes equations
  • Non-equilibrium fluid mechanics
  • Active systems
  • Charged and uncharged ring and linear polymers
