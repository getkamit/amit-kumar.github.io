About Me

My name is Amit Kumar. I am a PhD student at The Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai, India. I have just submitted my thesis and will soon be joining ICTS Bangalore for postdoctoral research.

My research has focused on investigating mechanical effects of shock propagation from intense explosions — a classic problem in gas dynamics — using molecular dynamics simulations and theoretical frameworks based on the Euler and Navier-Stokes equations.

I'm currently interested in extending this work and also exploring non-equilibrium fluid mechanics, active systems, charged/uncharged ring and linear polymers, and related areas.
